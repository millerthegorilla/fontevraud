LINKS: FONTEVRAUD L'ABBAYE

The village of Fontevraud is a small quiet village, that is not far from the two major towns of Saumur and Chinon. There are three good restaurants in the village, which also has a bakery, a butcher, a grocer, a florist, several bars and two tearooms. There is a doctor's surgery, a chemist, a post office and cash dispenser. There are leisure activities, golf (in the area), tennis at Montsoreau, canoe/kayak at Chinon, bike hire in Candes St Martin and also Horse riding trips such as those offered by Les Abrons.
